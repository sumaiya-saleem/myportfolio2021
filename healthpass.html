About HealthPass


Currently in Ontario, there is no way for patients to access their own electronic health records, and are required to contact each organization individually. HealthPass is an early-stage digital startup, and aims to empower patients to access their own health records, and enhance the retrieval and sharing process.

My Role


As a brand and product designer, I am currently working on curating the HealthPass brand identity, and also create user flows + high fidelity mockups for the product MVP.

While this case study is under construction, feel free to check out our website and social media to view updates and accomplishments. If you are interested in learning more or getting involved, contact me!
