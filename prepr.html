Overview

Prepr is a leading startup for open education platforms, allowing students to collaborate and solve real world challenges. During my internship, I was responsible for creating solutions for improving the visual design and content structure of the Prepr website and app, designing new features for the app, content creation, managing brand identity and product design.

Design Challenge

When applying for the role, the company issued a design challenge to redesign a new homepage, which was used as a starting point to the website's redesign. My design consisted of persona creation, content study, userflow, wireframes, and a lo-fidelity design. View my initial design challenge here.

Website UX Design and Development

Using Figma, we created numerous high-fidelity mockups, organizing them based on their site map hierarchy. After getting approval for each page, I asissted the developer in transorming some of these pages on Wordpress. To keep track of the status of pages, we created an excel file to audit all the content.

Mobile App UX Design

UI designed various features and user flows for the mobile app. I worked with the existing UI of the app, and had to describe the functionality of each step in the flow as this would be communicated to with an outsourced development team. The images below shows the user flow to purchase challenges, as well as a snippet of the mobile app tutorial.

Brand Design

Working alongside another designer, and leading two UX designer volunteers, I was appointed as the "brand guideline controller", to oversee that all pages have a consistent designs. We implemented a style guide that would allow for consistent branding throughout the Prepr Network pages.

Additional Projects

Throughout my time at Prepr, I worked on additional projects that were outside the internship scope. These projects included various challenge box product designs, activity books, brochures, sticker packs, pitch presentations, Apple Watch visioning, banners, and more.
