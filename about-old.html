Nice to meet you!

I am a data-driven UX designer, passionate about how good design can be used to positively impact different communities.

When I’m not designing, you can find me playing chess, painting, or trying out some new recipes.

If you would like to know more about my projects, or would like to chat over some coffee, let’s get in touch!
