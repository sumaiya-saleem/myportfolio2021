Objective


LeapGrad is a high growth startup, and is working on a building a student-focused, professional networking platform. As a UX researcher at LeapGrad, I am responsible for leading the UX research and design process to redesign the current interface, with the goal of improving the student job search and networking experience.

Process


  1. Defining assumptions and hypotheses, analyzing prior research and collected data
  2. Conducting primary and secondary research
  3. Conducting exploratory interviews with current users, their experience using the platfrom to understand needs, challenges and goals
  4. Synthesis and affinity mapping
  5. Defining the problem
  6. Solution ideation
  7. Designing wireframes and mockups
  8. Usability testing
